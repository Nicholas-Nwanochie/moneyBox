Our vision

Boxing is more than a sport here, its a family!

Enjoyment is the number one reason why anybody would ever got good at anything. The one thing boxing has over other fitness exercises is its enjoyment! At Artillery boxing we belive that if you plan on being successful at anything, it has to be fun for you. Otherwise, it becomes work, becomes a chore, and you’ll quit before you ever get going. This is the problem with most workouts, and this is what we aim on fixing .

Our vision

Boxing = Life

At artillery boxing we strive to make the boxing experince fun and enjoyable. From novice beginners who never put on gloves to elite fighters who have dozens of fights, we cater to them all. Thanks to the inhernt fun in boxing, falling in love with the sport comes easy. Getting good at it is the hard part, and that is where we at Artillery boxing come in. By supplying you with a safe and comfertable envirment to focus on your embetterment and health. We win when you win! And we aim to make evryone who walks though our doors a winner.

Classes offered

We offer class designed around boxing. All our workouts and cardio sessions will have a boxing base, but will have benefits for all sports. Sweat, fatigue, and completing goals are a few of the things you can expect from each and every class.
